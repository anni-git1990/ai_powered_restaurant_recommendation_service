## Brand & Style

The design system is engineered to evoke a sense of culinary excellence combined with the precision of high-end technology. It targets a discerning audience that seeks both convenience and quality, shifting the restaurant discovery experience from a manual search to a curated, intelligent concierge service.

The aesthetic blends **Modern Corporate** reliability with **Glassmorphism** accents to signify the "AI layer." This is achieved through a high-contrast white environment, punctuated by the signature crimson brand color. The interface remains appetizing by using generous whitespace, allowing high-quality food photography to serve as the primary visual driver. AI-driven insights are distinguished by soft-shaded backgrounds and subtle glass surfaces, creating a "lens" through which the user views recommendations.

## Colors

The color palette is anchored by **Crimson Red**, used purposefully for primary actions and brand presence. 

- **Primary (#E23744):** Reserved for the "Primary Call to Action," brand iconography, and critical interactive states.
- **Surface Tiers:** Use **Crisp White (#FFFFFF)** for the main content area and **Light Gray (#F8F8F8)** for secondary containers and background depth.
- **Typography:** **Dark Charcoal (#1C1C1C)** provides high-contrast legibility for headings, while **Slate Gray (#696969)** reduces visual noise for long-form body copy.
- **Accents:** **Golden Yellow (#FFBA00)** is used exclusively for ratings and achievement markers. A specific **Soft Pink/Red Tint** is used for glassmorphism background blurs to maintain brand warmth within transparent elements.

## Typography

The design system utilizes **Inter** across all levels to ensure a systematic, clean, and highly readable experience. 

- **Headlines:** Use Bold (700) and Semi-Bold (600) weights with slight negative letter spacing to create a premium, editorial feel. 
- **Body Text:** Standardized at 16px for optimal legibility. Use the Slate Gray color to maintain a soft visual hierarchy against the Dark Charcoal headings.
- **AI Insights:** Text generated by AI should utilize the `body-lg` scale with a slightly increased line-height to emphasize readability and "premium" guidance.
- **Mobile Scaling:** Headlines transition to smaller scales on mobile to ensure balanced line lengths and prevent excessive wrapping.

## Layout & Spacing

This design system follows a **12-column fluid grid** for desktop and a **single-column fluid layout** for mobile. 

- **Rhythm:** An 8px linear scale (with a 4px half-step) governs all padding and margins. 
- **Margins:** Desktop views utilize 40px global margins; mobile views drop to 16px to maximize screen real estate.
- **Sectioning:** Vertical spacing between major sections (e.g., "Recommended for You" vs "Cuisines") should use the `xl` (40px) token to maintain an airy, high-end feel.
- **AI Containers:** AI-powered cards and search blocks should utilize `lg` (24px) internal padding to allow the content to "breathe."

## Elevation & Depth

Hierarchy is established through a combination of **Ambient Shadows** and **Glassmorphism**.

1.  **Level 0 (Base):** Crisp White (#FFFFFF) background.
2.  **Level 1 (Cards):** Subtle, extra-diffused shadow (`0px 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`). Elements should appear to "rest" lightly on the surface.
3.  **Level 2 (Hover/Active):** On hover, cards lift physically using a more pronounced shadow (`0px 12px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`) and a subtle scale transform (1.02x).
4.  **Glassmorphism (AI Overlays):** Used for the primary search bar and AI suggestion modals. This requires a `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` with a semi-transparent white fill (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.8)`) and a 1px white border at 20% opacity.

## Shapes

The shape language is distinctly **Rounded**, conveying a friendly and approachable personality.

- **Standard Elements:** Buttons and small input fields use a **0.5rem (8px)** radius.
- **Featured Cards:** Restaurant and AI recommendation cards use a larger **1rem (16px)** radius for a modern look.
- **AI Elements:** Specific AI-driven components (like the search bar or "Magic" suggestions) can utilize the **rounded-xl (24px)** radius to feel distinct from the standard utility UI.
- **Badges:** Cuisine badges and "Open/Closed" tags should be fully pill-shaped (capsule) to contrast against the more structural card shapes.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Crimson Red fill, white text. High-contrast, no border.
- **Secondary:** White fill, 1px Gray border, Dark Charcoal text.
- **AI-Action:** Crimson Red to Deep Pink gradient (subtle) with a sparkle icon (✨).

### Restaurant Cards
- **Structure:** Full-width image at the top with a 16px corner radius. Content area below with `md` padding.
- **Interaction:** Soft lift on hover with a 200ms transition.
- **Badges:** Overlaid on the image top-left. Semi-transparent dark background for "AI Pick" or Cuisine type.

### Search & Inputs
- **Search Bar:** Large, centered, using glassmorphism effects. Internal padding of `lg`. Text weight for placeholder should be `body-md`.
- **Inputs:** Soft-gray background (#F8F8F8) with a 1px border that turns Crimson Red on focus.

### AI Sparkle Highlighting
- When the AI provides a "Why you'll like this" snippet, the container should use a **Soft Pink background (rgba(226, 55, 68, 0.05))** with a subtle left-accent border in Crimson Red. Include a small `label-sm` text with the sparkle icon (✨) to signal AI generation.

### Ratings
- Star ratings use **Golden Yellow (#FFBA00)**. Use solid stars for the rating and outlined stars for the remainder of the 5-point scale. Include the numeric value in `label-md` bold next to the stars.
